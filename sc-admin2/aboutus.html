Our Story

We Started as Frustrated Gamers

SoloCloud was born in 2023 out of frustration. Our founders — a group of passionate gamers and developers based in India — kept running into the same problems: slow servers, expensive pricing with hidden fees, support teams that took days to respond, and panels that felt like they were designed in 2009.

We knew the technology existed to do better. Enterprise NVMe SSDs, AMD EPYC processors, global data centres, and DDoS protection were all accessible — they just weren't being combined into a product that actually respected the customer.

So we built SoloCloud. A hosting platform designed from the ground up with performance, transparency, and community at its core. No fluff, no hidden fees, no corporate runaround — just fast, reliable servers at honest prices, backed by real humans who actually play games.

Today SoloCloud powers thousands of game servers, Discord bots, VPS instances, and domains across India, Singapore, and Germany — and we're just getting started.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Performance First

Every hardware decision, every network choice, every software optimisation is made with one goal: making your server as fast and stable as possible. We never cut corners.

Community Driven

Our community shapes our roadmap. New features, new game support, and pricing decisions are all influenced by feedback from real customers on our Discord server.

Radical Transparency

What you see is what you pay. No hidden fees, no surprise charges, no misleading "unlimited" claims. We publish our infrastructure status publicly and own our mistakes.

Real Support

When something goes wrong, you talk to a real human who understands hosting — not a bot or a copy-pasted knowledge base article. We care about fixing it.

Honest Pricing

We built SoloCloud because we were tired of paying too much for too little. Our prices are set to be genuinely affordable for students, streamers, and growing communities.

Always Reliable

99.9% uptime SLA across all services. DDoS protection on every server. Automated monitoring. Your community depends on your server — we take that seriously.
