07 — Guidelines

Logo Usage Rules

Follow these guidelines when using SoloCloud brand assets in any content or media.

Do
Use the logo with adequate clear space around it
Use the correct logo version for the background (dark / light)
Maintain original proportions when resizing
Use official brand colors from the palette
Credit SoloCloud when using assets in creator content
Use SVG files for print or large-format materials
Don't
Stretch, skew, or distort the logo in any way
Change the logo colors or add gradients not in the palette
Place the logo on busy backgrounds without a clear contrast
Add drop shadows, strokes, or effects to the logo
Use the logo to imply an official endorsement without permission
Recreate, trace, or modify the logo artwork
