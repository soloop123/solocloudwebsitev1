How to Pay — Quick Guide

Automatic Methods (INR & Crypto)
  1. Register or log in at billing.solocloud.host
  2. Choose your plan and click Order Now
  3. Select your preferred payment method at checkout
  4. Complete the payment — your service is activated instantly
Manual Methods (PKR / NPR / BDT / PayPal)
  1. Click any yellow "Via Ticket" payment method above
  2. Open a ticket via Discord or the billing portal
  3. Tell us which plan and payment method you want
  4. Our team sends you the payment details and activates your service after confirmation

Payment FAQs

Are refunds available?

Due to the nature of digital hosting services, all sales are final. No refunds are issued after a service is activated. Please review our Terms of Service for full details.

How long do manual payments take?

Manual ticket payments are typically processed within 1–6 hours during active support hours. Our team will confirm receipt and activate your service once payment is verified.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. Automatic payments are processed through our secure billing platform. We never store raw card or wallet credentials. Manual payments are handled privately via encrypted ticket channels.

Can I add balance to my account?

Yes. You can top up your billing account balance using any supported payment method and use it to pay for any service — including manual methods via ticket.

What currency are prices shown in?

All prices on the website and billing panel are displayed in Indian Rupees (INR). For manual payments in other currencies, our team will provide the equivalent conversion rate at the time of the transaction.

My payment method isn't listed — can I still pay?

If your preferred method isn't listed here, open a Discord or billing ticket and describe what you'd like to use. We try to accommodate new payment methods where possible.
