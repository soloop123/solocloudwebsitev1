What's Included

Enterprise Grade on Every Plan

Pure NVMe SSD Storage

Every VPS runs on enterprise NVMe SSDs delivering up to 3,500 MB/s read speed — no spinning HDDs, no shared SATA pools. Your applications boot fast, databases respond instantly, and file I/O never becomes a bottleneck even under heavy load.

⚡

Full Root Access

Total control over your environment. Install any software, configure the kernel, set up custom firewalls, and manage every aspect of your server exactly as you need.

DDoS Protection

Enterprise-grade multi-layer DDoS mitigation is included on every VPS plan at no extra charge — keeping your server reachable even under attack.

Snapshots & Backups

Take on-demand snapshots before risky updates. Starter plans get weekly scheduled backups; Pro and Elite get daily. Restore with one click from the panel.

1 Gbps Network

All plans include a 1 Gbps uplink port. High-bandwidth applications, game servers, and media streaming run smoothly without port-speed throttling.

3 Global Locations

Deploy in India, Singapore, or Germany — whichever is closest to your users. Migrate between regions at any time by raising a support ticket.
